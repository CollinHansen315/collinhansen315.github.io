About Collin Hansen

My name is Collin Hansen and I am a Sports Media & Communications Major at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am originally from Omaha, Nebraska but spent 17 years of my life growing up in Chandler, Arizona. I came back to Nebraska to go to college because I wanted to further my education and dive into a potential future in the world of sports media and sports data analytics. This website showcases my sports data analytics that I have completed during my time as a student at the University of Nebraska.
